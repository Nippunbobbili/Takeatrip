Manali,Himachal Pradesh

Enter an enticing universe in beautiful Manali. It is a mountainous hill station in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. There are wildflower meadows, small charming hamlets, and fruit-laden orchards surrounded by dense woods. There are several cottages with mountain views and a pure ambiance that are ideal for a honeymoon, as well as you will cherish many unforgettable experiences

Taj Mahal,Agra

Agra’s Taj Mahal is a magnificent example of Mughal architecture. Aside from being one of India’s most beautiful locations, the Taj Mahal is without a doubt the world’s largest and most daring romantic symbol and of course the best honeymoon destination in India.

Pondicherry,Tamil Nadu

Pondy, India’s “Little Paris,” is a faded colonial mansion with a touch of bohemian-chic. You’re in for a romantic stay in this François, creative, and spiritual city. Puducherry (also known as Pondicherry or simply Pondy)

Darjeeling,West Bengal

Only the name of this town conjures up the aroma of tea leaves. Darjeeling, in the Himalayan foothills, is one of the most romantic honeymoon destinations in India, with its curving rows of manicured tea bushes gracefully shaping the hilly scenery into emerald-green undulations.

Munnar,Kerala

Munnar, Kerala’s beautiful hill resort, is one of the most exotic honeymoon destinations in India. Munnar is carpeted in verdant tea plantations that have been curved, pruned, and molded like decorative hedges, and it is surrounded by ’50 shades of Green.’ Journey to a tranquil highland station. You will find boundless beauty here, away from the hustle and bustle.

ooty,Tamil Nadu

You can't really find a place as beautiful as Ooty in South India for your honeymoon. The little hill station in Tamil Nadu has gorgeous waterfalls, a calm lake

Nainital,Uttarakhand

Now, no one can challenge Nainital as a honeymoon destination. honeymoon couples round the year at this top tourist destination in india. Nainital is a Himalayan resort town in the Kumaon region of India’s Uttarakhand state

Udaipur,Rajasthan

Udaipur is proven as the best Royal romantic getaway in India for the Royal romance. This place is known for its palaces, temples, lakes, and forts.

Ananthagiri Hills,Telanagana

Ananthagiri Hills is located in Vikarabad district, Telangana, India. The water flows from these hills to Osman Sagar, also known as Gandipet lake, and Himayathsagar. It is one of the dense forests in Telangana. Ananthagiri Temple is located in this forested area.

shimla,Himachal pradesh

Shimla is the capital of the northern Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, in the Himalayan foothills. Once the summer capital of British India, it remains the terminus of the narrow-gauge Kalka-Shimla Railway, completed in 1903. It’s also known for the handicraft shops that line The Mall, a pedestrian avenue, as well as the Lakkar Bazaar, a market specializing in wooden toys and crafts.

Kodaikanal,Tamil Nadu

Everyone knows how beautiful Kodaikanal in Tamil Nadu is. This lovely hill station, therefore, is amongst the top places for honeymoon couples in India.

Shillong,Meghalaya

With stunning landscapes and silent mountains, Shillong would make for a beautiful honeymoon destination. The city is home to numerous waterfalls.
